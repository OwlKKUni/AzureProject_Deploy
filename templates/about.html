About Helldivers 2 Game Results Tracker

Welcome to the Helldivers 2 game results tracker project! This application is designed to help you keep track of your in-game performance, including individual round stats, dive logs, and overall performance metrics.

Features of the Helldivers 2 Game Results Tracker include:

  • Input and manage individual round information
  • View detailed dive logs
  • Access a comprehensive history of all dives
  • Display and sort performance tables
  • Analyze data to enhance gameplay tactics

I hope this tracker enhances your Helldivers 2 experience by providing valuable insights into your gameplay performance. Dive in and start tracking!
